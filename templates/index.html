Health is Wealth

The system uses Naive Bayesian classification techniques to predict disease. This information is collected from different patients and sources. However, the limitation of this model is that it treats each symptom independently, regardless of the patient's previous health history. Read More

Advancements for this Application

This advanced model can accurately predict 42 diseases based on the analysis of 132 symptoms exhibited by patients. Although it doesn't take into account the patients' previous medical history, it effectively serves patients who have no medical history available. The primary objective of this model is to bridge the gap between patients and doctors, enabling swift patient recovery. There are numerous opportunities for further advancements to enhance the functionality and user experience of the current model.
